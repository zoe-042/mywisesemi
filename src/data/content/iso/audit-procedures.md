
# Audit Procedures

## Internal Audit Program

### Objective
To verify the effectiveness of our Quality Management System and ensure compliance with ISO 9001:2015 requirements.

### Audit Planning
- Annual audit schedule development
- Audit team selection and training
- Audit criteria and scope definition
- Pre-audit preparation activities

### Audit Execution
1. Opening meeting with auditees
2. Document review and examination
3. Process observation and interviews
4. Evidence collection and analysis
5. Nonconformity identification
6. Closing meeting and report presentation

### Follow-up Activities
- Corrective action plan development
- Implementation verification
- Effectiveness review
- Continuous improvement recommendations

## External Audit Management

### Certification Audit Preparation
- Documentation review and updates
- Internal audit completion
- Management review execution
- Corrective action closure

### Surveillance Audit Support
- Audit coordination and logistics
- Evidence preparation and presentation
- Finding response and resolution
- Certificate maintenance activities
