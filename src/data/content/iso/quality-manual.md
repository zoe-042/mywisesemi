
# ISO 9001:2015 Quality Manual

## Introduction

This Quality Manual describes our Quality Management System (QMS) in accordance with ISO 9001:2015 standard requirements.

## Scope

This QMS applies to all operations within WiseSemi including design, development, production, and service delivery.

## Quality Policy

We are committed to providing high-quality semiconductor solutions that meet or exceed customer expectations while continuously improving our processes.

## Quality Objectives

- Achieve 99.9% product quality standards
- Maintain customer satisfaction above 95%
- Reduce defect rates by 10% annually
- Ensure on-time delivery of 98%

## Process Documentation

All processes are documented and regularly reviewed to ensure compliance with ISO 9001:2015 requirements.
