
# Employee Handbook

## Welcome

Welcome to our company! This handbook is designed to provide you with information about our policies, procedures, and benefits.

## Company Policies

- Work Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday
- Remote Work: Flexible remote work options available
- Time Off: 20 days of paid vacation annually, plus public holidays
- Sick Leave: 10 days of paid sick leave annually

## Benefits

- Health Insurance: Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision coverage
- Retirement: 401(k) with company matching up to 5%
- Professional Development: Annual budget of $2,000 for courses and conferences
- Wellness Program: Gym membership subsidy and mental health resources
