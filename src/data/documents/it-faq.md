
# IT Frequently Asked Questions

## Network Access

### How do I connect to the company WiFi?

Select the "WiseSemi-Secure" network and enter your employee ID as the username and your network password.

### I forgot my network password. What should I do?

Visit the IT Help Desk portal or contact the IT department at it.support@wisesemi.com.

## Hardware Support

### How do I request new equipment?

Submit a request through the IT Help Desk portal with your manager's approval.

### What should I do if my laptop is not working properly?

Restart your laptop first. If the issue persists, contact the IT Help Desk at extension 2500.

## Software

### How do I install company-approved software?

Access the Software Center from your desktop and select from available applications.

### How do I request access to specific software?

Submit a software access request form through the IT Help Desk portal.

## Security

### How often should I change my password?

Passwords must be changed every 90 days. You'll receive email reminders starting 14 days before expiration.

### What should I do if I suspect a security breach?

Disconnect your device from the network and immediately contact the IT Security team at security@wisesemi.com or extension 2525.

## Remote Access

### How do I access company resources when working remotely?

Use the VPN client installed on your laptop. Instructions for connection are available on the IT portal.

### I'm having trouble connecting to VPN. What should I do?

Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If issues persist, contact the IT Help Desk.
