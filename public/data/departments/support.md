
# Customer Support Department / 客戶服務部

The Customer Support department provides exceptional service to our clients, ensuring their needs are met and issues are resolved promptly.

客戶服務部為我們的客戶提供卓越的服務，確保滿足他們的需求並及時解決問題。

## Key Responsibilities / 主要職責

- Customer inquiry handling / 客戶詢問處理
- Technical support / 技術支援
- Issue resolution and escalation / 問題解決與升級
- Customer satisfaction monitoring / 客戶滿意度監控
- Product documentation / 產品文件
- Training and support materials / 培訓與支援材料
