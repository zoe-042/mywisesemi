
# Finance Department / 財務部

The Finance department oversees financial planning, accounting, budgeting, and financial reporting to ensure the company's fiscal health and compliance.

財務部負責監督財務規劃、會計、預算和財務報告，以確保公司的財務健康和合規性。

## Key Responsibilities / 主要職責

- Financial planning and analysis / 財務規劃與分析
- Budgeting and forecasting / 預算與預測
- Accounting and bookkeeping / 會計與記帳
- Financial reporting / 財務報告
- Cash flow management / 現金流管理
- Investment management / 投資管理
- Risk assessment and management / 風險評估與管理

- YP Fang, Finance Director / 財務總監

## Focus Areas / 重點領域

- Quarterly financial reporting / 季度財務報告
- Budget optimization / 預算優化
- Cost control initiatives / 成本控制計劃
- Investment strategy development / 投資策略發展

## Financial Policies / 財務政策

- Expense approval procedures / 費用審批程序
- Budget allocation guidelines / 預算分配指導原則
- Financial reporting standards / 財務報告標準
- Compliance requirements / 合規要求
