
# RD2 Department / 研發二部

The Research and Development 2 department specializes in product optimization, manufacturing process improvement, and quality enhancement.

研發二部專精於產品優化、製造流程改善和品質提升。

- Product optimization and enhancement / 產品優化與改良
- Manufacturing process development / 製造流程開發
- Quality assurance methodologies / 品質保證方法論
- Testing and validation / 測試與驗證
- Production support / 生產支援
- Continuous improvement initiatives / 持續改善計劃
