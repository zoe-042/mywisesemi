
# Operations Department / 營運部

The Operations department ensures efficient day-to-day business processes, supply chain management, and operational excellence across the organization.

營運部確保高效的日常業務流程、供應鏈管理和整個組織的營運卓越。

## Key Responsibilities / 主要職責

- Supply chain management / 供應鏈管理
- Process optimization / 流程優化
- Quality control / 品質控制
- Inventory management / 庫存管理
- Vendor relationship management / 供應商關係管理
- Operational planning and strategy / 營運規劃與策略

- Cost reduction target: 10% / 成本降低目標：10%
- Process efficiency improvement: 15% / 流程效率改善：15%

## Key Processes / 關鍵流程

- Manufacturing coordination / 製造協調
- Logistics and distribution / 物流與配送
- Quality assurance procedures / 品質保證程序
- Supplier evaluation and management / 供應商評估與管理
