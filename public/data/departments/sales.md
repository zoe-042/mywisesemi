
# Sales Department / 業務部

The Sales department drives revenue growth through strategic customer relationships, market expansion, and effective sales processes.

業務部通過戰略客戶關係、市場擴張和有效的銷售流程推動收入增長。

## Key Responsibilities / 主要職責

- Lead generation and qualification / 潛在客戶開發與資格審查
- Customer relationship management / 客戶關係管理
- Sales pipeline management / 銷售管道管理
- Market analysis and strategy / 市場分析與策略
- Contract negotiation / 合約談判
- Revenue forecasting / 收入預測
