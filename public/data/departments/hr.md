
# Human Resources Department / 人力資源部

The Human Resources department is responsible for managing employee relations, recruitment, benefits administration, and organizational development.

人力資源部負責管理員工關係、招聘、福利管理和組織發展。

## Key Responsibilities / 主要職責

- Talent acquisition and recruitment / 人才招募與錄用
- Employee onboarding and training / 員工入職與培訓
- Benefits and compensation management / 福利與薪酬管理
- Performance management / 績效管理
- Employee relations and conflict resolution / 員工關係與衝突解決
- Policy development and compliance / 政策制定與合規
- Organizational development / 組織發展
