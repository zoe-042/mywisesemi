
# Marketing Department / 行銷部

The Marketing department is responsible for promoting our brand, driving customer engagement, and supporting business growth through strategic marketing initiatives.

行銷部負責推廣我們的品牌，推動客戶參與，並通過戰略行銷舉措支持業務增長。

## Key Responsibilities / 主要職責

- Brand management and positioning / 品牌管理與定位
- Digital marketing and social media / 數位行銷與社群媒體
- Content creation and marketing campaigns / 內容創作與行銷活動
- Market research and analysis / 市場研究與分析
- Customer relationship management / 客戶關係管理
- Product launch and promotion / 產品發布與推廣
- Partnership and collaboration development / 合作夥伴關係發展
