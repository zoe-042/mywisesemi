
# 節慶活動 / Holiday Events

## 公司特別節慶 Company Special Events

### 公司週年慶 Company Anniversary
每年慶祝公司成立週年，回顧過去，展望未來。

Annual celebration of company founding with reflection and future planning.

### 員工表彰大會 Employee Recognition Ceremony
表彰優秀員工，激勵團隊士氣。

Recognizing outstanding employees and boosting team morale.

### 春節聯歡會 Chinese New Year Celebration
傳統的春節聚會，包含特色表演和團圓晚餐。

Traditional Chinese New Year gathering with cultural performances and reunion dinner.

### 中秋節活動 Mid-Autumn Festival
賞月活動，月餅分享，團隊聯誼。

Moon-watching activities, mooncake sharing, and team bonding.

### 聖誕節慶祝 Christmas Celebration
年末聖誕聚會，禮物交換，感恩回顧。

Year-end Christmas party with gift exchange and gratitude reflection.
